Culture & Heritage of Melaka

Melaka is a melting pot of cultures shaped by centuries of trade, migration, and colonial history. Its living heritage continues to thrive through traditions, architecture, cuisine, and festivals.

Baba Nyonya

The Baba Nyonya community represents a unique fusion of Chinese and Malay cultures. Their traditions are reflected in their language, customs, architecture, and distinctive cuisine passed down through generations.

Peranakan Heritage

Peranakan culture is known for its colourful kebaya, intricate beadwork, porcelain designs, and rich culinary heritage that blends Eastern and local flavours.

Traditional Arts & Music

Melaka preserves traditional performances such as Dondang Sayang, kompang, and traditional dances that symbolise harmony and community spirit.

Jonker Walk Night Market

Held every weekend, Jonker Walk Night Market showcases Melaka‚Äôs vibrant street culture through local food, crafts, performances, and cultural activities.

Colonial Architecture

Historic buildings influenced by Portuguese, Dutch, and British rule remain well preserved, giving Melaka its distinctive architectural identity.

Festivals & Celebrations

Cultural festivals such as Chinese New Year, Hari Raya, Deepavali, and heritage celebrations highlight Melaka‚Äôs multicultural harmony.
